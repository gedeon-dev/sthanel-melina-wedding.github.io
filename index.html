Our Story

It all began in May 2024, though neither of us knew it yet.
Sthanel, a young French man, was seeking God’s will for his life. In prayer, he received one word: “army.” That led him to begin the recruitment process for the French military. Around the same time, he discovered a Bible school in Kilgoris, Kenya, through the website of his church, SOS Lyon, where he had been attending for a year. His pastor offered to speak with him about it after service.

That same Sunday, Milana, a missionary from the U.S., came to preach at SOS Lyon. She had just come through a difficult season in Kenya and was sent to Europe to speak in the SOS churches across Switzerland and France.

Our StoryThe first exchanges

Though they were in the same church that day, they didn’t meet. Later, when Milana saw the new Bible school students, one face stood out: Sthanel’s. Something about him caught her attention. When he arrived in Kenya, Sthanel saw Milana holding a child and thought she was a mom. At that exact moment, Milana thought, “I should give this child back before he thinks they’re mine.” Their first real interaction came soon after. Milana asked: – What made you come to Kenya? Sthanel answered simply: – You’re the reason I’m here. Milana was stunned, but held it in… and walked away smiling. Over the following months, they built a friendship, served Jesus together, and something beautiful began to grow.

The proposal

Then, on December 25, 2024, in the picturesque village of Saint-Antonin-Noble-Val in France, overlooking a cliff, Sthanel proposed. Milana said yes. Now they’re back in Kenya — Milana continuing her mission, and Sthanel finished MBS and LA — as they prepare for the next chapter… A wedding by the beach in Mombasa.
